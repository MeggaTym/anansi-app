How to Play

AnansiApp is a mathematical challenge requring some level of thinking. The aim of the game is to remove the spiders surrounding the grid, and to do this;
You're a given a 3x3 grid in which values 1-9 can be inserted, and no duplicates are allowed. The spiders are removed from each row and/column only if the sum of the 3 values in the row/column equals to 15.
On Level 2, the sum diagonally should be equal to 15

Colors and Objects

The red color means that the current entered value is a duplicate.
The green means the grid is valid and complete.
The spiders around the grid mean the row/column sum is not 15
